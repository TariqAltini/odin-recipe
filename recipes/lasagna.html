Lasagna - Odin Edition

Description

Making lasagna has never been easier, this recipe guarantees the best flavour!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
